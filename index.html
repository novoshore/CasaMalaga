The house

A beautifully restored Andalusian townhouse in Málaga's historic heart. High ceilings, terracotta tiles, and a private courtyard make it a genuine city sanctuary for up to 6 guests.

Bedrooms

Two bright double rooms with quality bedding, natural light, and built-in storage.

Living & Kitchen

Open-plan living with fully equipped modern kitchen. Everything you need to cook and relax.
